How AERO Works

🚗
01
ACCIDENT DETECTION
Multi-axis accelerometers and gyroscopes continuously monitor vehicle dynamics. Sudden deceleration exceeding threshold G-forces, combined with airbag deployment signals, trigger the detection algorithm.
ACCELEROMETER · GYROSCOPE · AIRBAG SENSOR
📍
02
GPS LOCATION CAPTURE
High-precision GPS module instantly locks the exact coordinates of the incident, cross-referenced with cellular triangulation for accuracy in GPS-shadowed areas like tunnels and urban canyons.
GPS MODULE · CELLULAR TRIANGULATION · ±2M ACCURACY
📡
03
AUTOMATIC EMERGENCY ALERT
Within milliseconds of detection, an encrypted alert packet containing GPS coordinates, vehicle ID, impact severity score, and timestamp is transmitted over 4G/5G to the Central Emergency Hub.
4G/5G · MQTT PROTOCOL · ENCRYPTED PAYLOAD
🚑
04
NEAREST AMBULANCE DISPATCH
The system queries the real-time ambulance fleet database, identifies the closest available unit using live traffic-adjusted routing, and automatically dispatches with turn-by-turn navigation to the accident site.
FLEET MANAGEMENT · REAL-TIME ROUTING · AUTO-DISPATCH
👨‍👩‍👧
05
FAMILY EMERGENCY ALERT
Registered emergency contacts receive simultaneous SMS and push notifications containing the accident location, a live tracking link, and the responding hospital's contact details.
SMS · PUSH NOTIFICATION · LIVE TRACKING LINK
🚦
06
SMART TRAFFIC SIGNAL CONTROL
Smart city infrastructure integration enables green corridor creation along the ambulance's predicted route. Traffic signals automatically switch to grant uninterrupted passage, cutting response time by up to 40%.
V2I COMMS · SMART CITY API · GREEN CORRIDOR

🤖
ZERO HUMAN INTERVENTION
Fully automated from impact detection through dispatch. The system acts within 8 seconds — far faster than any human could dial emergency services.

DUAL-MODE LOCATION
Primary GPS combined with cellular triangulation backup ensures location accuracy even in tunnels, underground garages, and signal-weak rural zones.
